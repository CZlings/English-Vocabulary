- _Personal growth_
  * Cultivate talents with comprehensive qualities
    > - Develop students' communication, teamwork, and leadership skills
    > - Inspire the interest and ability for lifelong learning
    > - Enhance problem solving skills and critical thinking
  * Achieve personalized growth
    > - Provide diverse course options based on students' interests
    > - Respect students' uniqueness and diversity
    > - Encourage students to set and achieve personal goals
- _Societal demands_
   * Meet labor market demands for high skilleds workers
     > - Offer vocational training programs aligned with market needs
     > - Increase the supply of talent for technological innovation
     > - Enhance students' employ ability and entrepreneurial skills
   * Narrow educational equity gaps
     > - Expand educational resources in remote areas
     > - Ensure equal educational opportunities regardless of gender and socioeconomic background
 - **Measurement**
 - _Education resources optimization_
   * Improve the evaluation system
     > - Include student's holistic qualities in evaluation criteria
     > - Encourage schools to adopt more non-exam-oriented education models
   * Promote curriculum diversification and practical application
     > - Increase financial subsidies
     > - Offer more scholarships and student loans for low income families
     > - Encourage businesses to donate to educational funds and support public welfare projects

  * Increase financial subsidies
    > - Offer more scholarships and student loans for low income families
    > - Encourage businesses to donates to educational funds and support public welfare projects
  * Establish a multicultural education system
    > Strengthen education in minority languages and cultures
    > Facilitate cultural exchange and understanding between domestic and international students
- **Pros / advantages**
- _Individual_
  * Enhance knowledge and skills
    > Gain systematic academic knowledge and practical skills
    > Improve foreign language proficiency for future globalization
    > Strengthen vocational skills through educational programs
  * Personal growth and well-rounded development
    > Cultivate interest in learning and foster self-learning habits
    > Develop students' skills in arts, sports, and other holistic areas
    > Boost self-confidence and sense of achievement
 - _Society_
   * Drive economic and social progress
     > Promote technological innovation and advancement
     > Reduce income inequality and enhance social equity
     > Boost national competitiveness and elevate international standing
   * Enhance cultural and value inheritance
     > Preserve and spread traditional culture through education
     > Enhance civic awareness and social responsibilityf

    > Students in impoverished areas lack access to quality education resources

    > Significant differences in urban and rural education levels

  ### Enviroment
  - **Purpose**
    * Maintain ecological balance
      > Prevent environmental issues such as desertification and forest degradation
      > Protect the natural ecological chain to ensure the coexistence of species
    * Protect biodiversity
      > Prevent the extinction of endangered species
      > Create nature reserves and wildlife habitats
    * improve quality of life
      > Reduce the harm of air pollution on health
      > Provide clean water sources and pollution-free soil
    * Reduce the impact of natural disasters
      > Reduce the frequency of floods and landslides through environmental management
      > Enhance the adapt ability of ecosystems to climate change
  - **Measurement**
    * _policies and regulations_
    * Strengthen environmental legislation
      > Establish stricter emission standards
      > Impose harsher penalties for violations
    * Promote international environmental cooperation
      > Sign global environmental agreements and share technological achievements
      > Coordinate transboundary environmental governance and pollution control
     * _Economy and Technology_
     * Promote clean energy
       > Provide financial subsidies for new energy development
       > Encourage companies and households to adopt clean energy
     * Optimize resource allocation
       > Increase investments in heavily polluted areas
       > Encourage public participation in community environmental projects
  - Pros 
     * Promote low-carbon technology and product innovation
  - Cons
    * _Economy_
    * High environmental protection costs
      > Large-scale environmental projects require significant investment
      > Companies bear additional costs for pollution control
    * Impaction traditional industries
      > Restrict the development of high-polluting industries
      > Transition may result in job reductions in traditional sectors
    * _Society_
    * Lack of public environmental awareness
      > Some regions lack awareness of environmental importance
      > Environmental projects may face public resistance
    * Uneven development of environmental technology
      > Developing countries struggle to afford environmental technologies
      > Environmental recovery has a long and slow impact cycle
