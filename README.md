#### Singular vs. Plural



* Noun: takes the place
* Pronoun: 
  - People/Animals: man, woman, cows
  - Places: house, playground
  - Things(Abstract or Concrete): idea, job
  - Reflexive Pronouns: himself, themselves

- Adjective: describes or modifies a noun
  - Actions: play, see, take, swim
  - Opposites: good, bad, long
